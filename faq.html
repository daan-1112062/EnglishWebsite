Frequently Asked Questions

What is Storytime Haven? +
Storytime Haven is a platform dedicated to bringing joy and education to children in hospitals through engaging and magical stories, games, and activities.
Who is Lumi? +
Lumi is our magical guide who helps children explore the world of learning in fun and creative ways. He’s a holographic owl with a big heart and a love for adventure!
How can I participate? +
If you’re a parent, guardian, or hospital staff member, reach out to us through our contact page to learn how to bring the Hospital Classroom experience to your location.
Is Storytime Haven free to use? +
Yes, Storytime Haven is free to use for children in hospitals. We are committed to making our content accessible to everyone.
What kind of activities do you offer? +
We offer interactive stories, educational games, fun quizzes, and creative activities designed to entertain and inspire children.
