Mia

Mia is a curious girl who loves to learn new things. Even though she’s in the hospital, she goes on amazing adventures to learn about space and more. She loves helping her friends discover the fun in learning!

Sam

Sam loves to draw and create cool things. At first, he didn’t think he liked school, but with some help from his friends, he learned that art and learning can be super fun when you do them together!

Dr. Theo

Dr. Theo is a kind doctor who created the Hospital Classroom to help kids keep learning while they're in the hospital. He’s always there to help with a big smile and lots of encouragement!

Lumi

Lumi is a magical owl who guides Mia and her friends on their learning adventures. With Lumi, kids can fly through space, learn about stars, and have fun exploring new things with every flap of his wings!
